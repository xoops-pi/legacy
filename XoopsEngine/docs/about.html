About Xoops Engine

XOOPS is an open-source web application system written in PHP. It is an ideal tool for developing small to large dynamic websites, including but not limited to intra company portals, corporate portals, weblogs and much more. The Xoops Engine designed and developed as next generation of XOOPS, has turned into an extensible application engine for web and mobile.

Xoops Engine is released under the terms of the GNU General Public License (GPL) and is free to use and modify. It is free to redistribute as long as you abide by the distribution terms of Xoops Engine License.

Xoops Engine is developed at Sourceforge.net and is supported at XoopsEngine.org. More information for XOOPS can be found at xoops.org.
